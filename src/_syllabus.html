Learning objectives

By the end of this class, you should be able to
  1. Articulate the guarantees of formal verification
  2. Write and understand specifications
  3. Verify simple programs in an imperative language

Assignments

There will be two programming exercises in Coq, one written theory assignment, and a final project in Coq.
